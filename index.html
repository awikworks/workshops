Low Tech-High Five!

Cicadra - Insect Synthesizer

2024 - Rokko Meet Arts, Kobe, Japan.

Starting from anmed "Modular Miskin." However, over time, the name was felt to bring misfortune, leading to its change to "Awikworks" with the motto "low tech-high five." Equipped with open-source modules, Muzeian continued to develop his DIY synthesizer. In addition to purchasing electronic components directly, he often dismantled and modified various used electronic devices to be repurposed into new instruments.

From this project, a sub-project called "AMPRRRONG" was born. Through AMPRRRONG, he recorded an EP in 2020, released by Anti Trust Record under the title Salamander Kodok Amprong: BAB I â€“ The Shell Creatures. By 2023, the exploration of Awikworks Modular had entered its fourth stage of evolution, where several modules were assembled into a single compact unit.
